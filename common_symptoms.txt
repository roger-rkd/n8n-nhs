================================================================================
50 COMMON DISEASES IN THE UK: SYMPTOMS, MEDICATIONS, AND HOME REMEDIES
================================================================================
Note: This list is for informational purposes. Always consult a pharmacist or 
the NHS (111) before starting new medications.
================================================================================

1. COMMON COLD
   - Symptoms: Sneezing, sore throat, cough, blocked/runny nose.
   - Medications: Paracetamol, Ibuprofen, Decongestant sprays.
   - Home Remedies: Rest, stay hydrated, steam inhalation.

2. INFLUENZA (FLU)
   - Symptoms: High temperature, exhaustion, body aches, dry cough.
   - Medications: Flu capsules (Lemsip), Paracetamol.
   - Home Remedies: Keep warm, sleep, drink plenty of water.

3. COVID-19
   - Symptoms: High temperature, continuous cough, loss of taste/smell.
   - Medications: Paracetamol for fever.
   - Home Remedies: Rest, stay hydrated, monitor breathing.

4. ASTHMA
   - Symptoms: Wheezing, breathlessness, tight chest.
   - Medications: Reliever inhalers (Salbutamol).
   - Home Remedies: Avoid triggers (smoke/pollen), breathing exercises.

5. HAY FEVER
   - Symptoms: Sneezing, itchy/watery eyes, runny nose.
   - Medications: Antihistamines (Cetirizine), Steroid nasal sprays.
   - Home Remedies: Vaseline around nostrils, wear wraparound sunglasses.

6. ECZEMA (ATOPIC)
   - Symptoms: Itchy, dry, cracked, and sore skin.
   - Medications: Emollients, Hydrocortisone cream (OTC).
   - Home Remedies: Avoid harsh soaps, pat skin dry, use cotton clothing.

7. TYPE 2 DIABETES
   - Symptoms: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, tiredness.
   - Medications: Metformin (Prescription).
   - Home Remedies: Low-sugar diet, regular exercise, weight management.

8. HYPERTENSION (HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE)
   - Symptoms: Usually none; severe cases include headaches/blurred vision.
   - Medications: ACE inhibitors (Ramipril), Calcium channel blockers.
   - Home Remedies: Reduce salt intake, quit smoking, reduce alcohol.

9. ARTHRITIS (OSTEOARTHRITIS)
   - Symptoms: Joint pain, stiffness, swelling.
   - Medications: Ibuprofen gel, Capsaicin cream, Paracetamol.
   - Home Remedies: Gentle exercise, hot/cold packs, weight loss.

10. ANXIETY DISORDER
    - Symptoms: Feeling restless, fast heartbeat, trembling.
    - Medications: Herbal remedies (Valerian root), Beta-blockers (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Deep breathing techniques, mindfulness, reduced caffeine.

11. DEPRESSION
    - Symptoms: Persistent low mood, loss of interest, low energy.
    - Medications: St John's Wort (OTC), SSRIs (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Regular exercise, sticking to a routine, talk therapy.

12. MIGRAINE
    - Symptoms: Severe throbbing headache, light sensitivity, nausea.
    - Medications: Sumatriptan (Pharmacy), Migraleve, Ibuprofen.
    - Home Remedies: Rest in a dark, quiet room; cold compress on forehead.

13. IRRITABLE BOWEL SYNDROME (IBS)
    - Symptoms: Stomach cramps, bloating, diarrhoea, constipation.
    - Medications: Buscopan (cramps), Peppermint oil capsules.
    - Home Remedies: Keep a food diary, cook from scratch, reduce stress.

14. GASTROENTERITIS (STOMACH BUG)
    - Symptoms: Sudden diarrhoea, vomiting, mild fever.
    - Medications: Rehydration salts (Dioralyte), Imodium (Loperamide).
    - Home Remedies: Sip water constantly, eat bland foods (rice/toast).

15. URINARY TRACT INFECTION (UTI)
    - Symptoms: Pain when peeing, needing to pee suddenly.
    - Medications: Cystitis sachets, Paracetamol.
    - Home Remedies: Drink plenty of water, avoid sugary drinks.

16. ACID REFLUX (GORD)
    - Symptoms: Heartburn, unpleasant taste, difficulty swallowing.
    - Medications: Gaviscon, Rennies, Omeprazole.
    - Home Remedies: Eat smaller meals, lose weight, avoid eating before bed.

17. TONSILLITIS
    - Symptoms: Sore throat, difficulty swallowing, swollen tonsils.
    - Medications: Throat lozenges, Ibuprofen.
    - Home Remedies: Gargle with warm salt water, drink cool liquids.

18. CHICKENPOX
    - Symptoms: Itchy spots, blisters, high temperature.
    - Medications: Calamine lotion, Antihistamines for itching.
    - Home Remedies: Keep nails short, wear loose cotton clothes.

19. GLANDULAR FEVER
    - Symptoms: Severe sore throat, swollen glands, extreme tiredness.
    - Medications: Paracetamol, Ibuprofen.
    - Home Remedies: Complete rest, plenty of fluids, avoid heavy lifting.

20. NOROVIRUS
    - Symptoms: Projectile vomiting, watery diarrhoea.
    - Medications: Rehydration salts.
    - Home Remedies: Wash hands with soap (gels don't work), bleach surfaces.

21. HIGH CHOLESTEROL
    - Symptoms: Typically no symptoms.
    - Medications: Statins (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Increase fiber (oats), reduce saturated fats.

22. COPD
    - Symptoms: Increasing breathlessness, persistent chesty cough.
    - Medications: Steroid inhalers (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Stop smoking immediately, breathing techniques.

23. ANAEMIA (IRON DEFICIENCY)
    - Symptoms: Tiredness, lack of energy, pale skin.
    - Medications: Iron supplements (Ferrous fumarate).
    - Home Remedies: Eat iron-rich foods (spinach, red meat, fortified cereals).

24. CATARACTS
    - Symptoms: Blurred or misty vision, faded colours.
    - Medications: None (Surgery required).
    - Home Remedies: Use brighter reading lights, wear sunglasses.

25. PSORIASIS
    - Symptoms: Red, flaky, crusty patches with silvery scales.
    - Medications: Coal tar shampoo, Vitamin D analogues.
    - Home Remedies: Moisturise daily, reduce stress, moderate sun exposure.

26. ANGINA
    - Symptoms: Chest pain (tight/heavy feeling) during exertion.
    - Medications: GTN spray (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Stop and rest immediately when pain occurs.

27. SINUSITIS
    - Symptoms: Pain/tenderness around cheeks, blocked nose.
    - Medications: Decongestant nasal sprays, Paracetamol.
    - Home Remedies: Apply a warm flannel to the face, stay hydrated.

28. CONJUNCTIVITIS (PINK EYE)
    - Symptoms: Red, gritty eyes; sticky discharge.
    - Medications: Antibiotic eye drops (Chloramphenicol).
    - Home Remedies: Clean eyelids with boiled (cooled) water and cotton wool.

29. GOUT
    - Symptoms: Sudden, severe joint pain (often big toe), redness.
    - Medications: Naproxen (Prescription), Ibuprofen.
    - Home Remedies: Avoid alcohol and offal, drink plenty of water.

30. HYPOTHYROIDISM (UNDERACTIVE THYROID)
    - Symptoms: Tiredness, weight gain, feeling cold.
    - Medications: Levothyroxine (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Ensure adequate iodine intake (via diet).

31. HYPERTHYROIDISM (OVERACTIVE THYROID)
    - Symptoms: Weight loss, heat sensitivity, anxiety.
    - Medications: Carbimazole (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Reduce caffeine and nicotine.

32. SHINGLES
    - Symptoms: Painful rash on one side, tingling, blisters.
    - Medications: Antiviral tablets (Prescription), Paracetamol.
    - Home Remedies: Wear loose clothing, use a cool compress.

33. SCARLET FEVER
    - Symptoms: Sandpaper-like rash, sore throat, red tongue.
    - Medications: Antibiotics (Prescription), Paracetamol.
    - Home Remedies: Soft foods, cool drinks, rest.

34. LYME DISEASE
    - Symptoms: Circular "bullseye" rash, flu-like symptoms.
    - Medications: Antibiotics (Doxycycline - Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Check for ticks after walks, use tweezers to remove ticks.

35. COELIAC DISEASE
    - Symptoms: Diarrhoea, stomach aches, bloating after eating gluten.
    - Medications: Vitamin supplements (if deficient).
    - Home Remedies: Strict lifelong gluten-free diet.

36. ENDOMETRIOSIS
    - Symptoms: Pelvic pain, heavy periods, pain during sex.
    - Medications: Ibuprofen, Hormonal contraceptives (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Heat pads/hot water bottles, TENS machines.

37. PROSTATE ENLARGEMENT
    - Symptoms: Difficulty starting to pee, weak flow.
    - Medications: Tamsulosin (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Limit fluid intake in the evening.

38. STROKE
    - Symptoms: Face dropping, arm weakness, speech difficulty.
    - Medications: Blood thinners (Hospital setting).
    - Home Remedies: CALL 999 IMMEDIATELY - No home remedy.

39. MEASLES
    - Symptoms: High temperature, white spots in mouth, blotchy rash.
    - Medications: Paracetamol for fever.
    - Home Remedies: Close curtains to reduce light sensitivity, rest.

40. MUMPS
    - Symptoms: Painful swellings at the side of the face.
    - Medications: Ibuprofen.
    - Home Remedies: Apply cold compresses to the swollen area.

41. WHOOPING COUGH
    - Symptoms: Intense coughing bouts followed by a "whoop" sound.
    - Medications: Antibiotics (if caught early).
    - Home Remedies: Use a humidifier, keep upright during coughs.

42. SEPSIS
    - Symptoms: Confusion, extreme shivering, mottled skin.
    - Medications: IV Antibiotics (Hospital only).
    - Home Remedies: CALL 999 IMMEDIATELY - No home remedy.

43. GALLSTONES
    - Symptoms: Sudden, severe pain in upper right tummy.
    - Medications: Strong painkillers (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Low-fat diet to prevent future attacks.

44. GLAUCOMA
    - Symptoms: Blurred vision, loss of side vision.
    - Medications: Prescription eye drops (Latanoprost).
    - Home Remedies: Regular eye tests are vital for prevention.

45. OSTEOPOROSIS
    - Symptoms: Usually no symptoms until a bone breaks.
    - Medications: Bisphosphonates, Calcium/Vitamin D tablets.
    - Home Remedies: Weight-bearing exercise (walking), quit smoking.

46. VERTIGO
    - Symptoms: Feeling like the world is spinning, dizziness.
    - Medications: Prochlorperazine (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Move slowly, lie down in a dark room.

47. PNEUMONIA
    - Symptoms: Chesty cough, difficulty breathing, fever.
    - Medications: Antibiotics (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Rest, stay hydrated, avoid smoking.

48. MENINGITIS
    - Symptoms: Stiff neck, light sensitivity, non-fading rash.
    - Medications: IV Antibiotics (Hospital only).
    - Home Remedies: CALL 999 IMMEDIATELY - No home remedy.

49. TINNITUS
    - Symptoms: Ringing, buzzing, or humming in the ears.
    - Medications: None (Management focus).
    - Home Remedies: Sound therapy (white noise), relaxation techniques.

50. SCIATICA
    - Symptoms: Pain, tingling, or numbness from back to leg.
    - Medications: Ibuprofen, Voltarol gel, Amitriptyline (Prescription).
    - Home Remedies: Gentle stretching, staying active, heat packs.
